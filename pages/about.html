Who We Are

Chennai-Born. Globally Delivered.

HNBC Software Solutions Pvt Ltd was founded with one conviction: that enterprise software should be an accelerator, not a constraint. Headquartered in Chennai — India's technology corridor — we combine deep engineering expertise with sharp business acumen to deliver software that drives real, measurable outcomes.

From nimble startups to Fortune 500 enterprises, we have partnered with organisations across six industries to modernise legacy systems, migrate to the cloud, embed AI into core operations, and build the digital products that define their competitive edge.
